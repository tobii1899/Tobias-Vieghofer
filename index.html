Diploma Thesis – Synthetic Data Generation for AI Training

I wrote my diploma thesis in cooperation with KPMG. The project was structured into two teams: an Analysis team and a Synthesis team. Together with a fellow student, I was part of the Analysis team. Our task was to analyze real datasets provided by KPMG, as their AI models could not be trained directly on sensitive real data. Based on the results of our analysis, synthetic datasets were generated, which can then be used safely for training artificial intelligence systems.

Cognitiva AI

Cognitiva AI is a non-medical software project aimed at supporting elderly care facilities by tracking cognitive changes over time. The system uses cognitive exercises to observe potential improvements or declines in cognitive performance. Based on user interaction and exercise results, progression is tracked in the background. In the future, AI-based analysis and recommendations (such as adjusting exercise intensity) are planned, although advanced AI integration is not yet part of the project.
